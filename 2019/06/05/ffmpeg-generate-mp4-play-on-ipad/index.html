ffmpeg_generate_mp4_play_on_ipad

Recently, i meet a problem that the video generated by ffmpeg cannot play on ipad

Symptoms

  1. I can paly the video generated by ffmpeg in the website on the PC broswer .
  2. But when i am try to play the video from broswer (safari or chrome) inside a n iPad, it doesn’t work.
  3. I transfer the video to ipad, and run it, sometimes it doesn’t work as well.

These ways i have tried

  1. When i meet the problem, i think it was concerned with the website plugin DPlayer, but the DPlayer demo video(download to local) worked well.
  2. I copy the video from PC to iPad and find that sometimes it doesn’t work well, the video cannot play or only paly one frame or green mask, so i’m sure that it caused by the video.
  3. I tryed to adjust ffmpeg parameter,eg :pix_fmt,vcodec,c:v,profile:v,level. Thay all didn’t work.
  4. I generated a video with other images, and the video could play from the broswer inside my iPad, and i found that the key point is the video size.

Diagnose

Because iPad not support all the varieties of MP4, and i found these specifications:

  • H.264 video (up to 720p, 30 frames per second; main profile level 3.1 with AAC-LC audio up to 160 Kbps, 48kHz, stereo audio in .m4v, .mp4, and .mov file formats)
  • MPEG-4 video, up to 2.5 Mbps, 640 by 480 pixels, 30 frames per second, Simple Profile with AAC-LC audio up to 160 Kbps, 48kHz, stereo audio in .m4v, .mp4, and .mov file formats.

Solution

Reduce the video size when generate
